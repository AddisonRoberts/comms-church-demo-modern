You're welcome here.

Hi, I'm Dave. Whatever brought you here — curiosity, a hard season, or a nudge you can't quite explain — we're glad you came. Ridgeline is full of ordinary people learning that life's mess is easier to carry together. Come as you are on Sunday, and we'll save you a seat.

More About Us

What to expect on a Sunday

  • What's the service like?

    Live music, a practical message from the Bible, and about 70 minutes total. Two gatherings: 9:00 classic, 11:00 contemporary.

  • What about my kids?

    Ridgeline Kids (birth–5th grade) meets during both gatherings. Secure check-in opens 20 minutes early at the Kids Wing desk.

  • What should I wear?

    Come as you are. Jeans, boots, or Sunday best — you'll fit right in.

  • Will I be singled out?

    No. You won't be asked to stand, speak, or give anything. Explore at your own pace.

Miss a Sunday? Catch up anytime.
