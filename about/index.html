About Ridgeline

Ordinary people, honest questions, and a God who meets us in the mess.

Why Ridgeline exists

Ridgeline started with a simple conviction: nobody should have to carry a hard season alone. We're a non-denominational church in Cedar Falls — ordinary people with real jobs, real families, and real questions, learning to follow Jesus together.

You don't need to have your beliefs sorted out to belong here. Come skeptical, come hurting, come curious. We'll meet you where you are.
